# Privacy Policy for MINDURA

**Last updated: February 10, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how MINDURA ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for the Service, specifically:
- Name
- Student/Parent Identification Numbers (NISN/NIK)
- Contact information (phone number, email)
- Address
- Date of Birth

### 1.2 Files and Documents
Our Service allows you to upload documents and images (e.g., student photos, birth certificates, family cards) for administrative purposes. These files are securely stored on our servers and are used solely for school registration and management verification.

### 1.3 Device Information
We may collect information about your mobile device, including:
- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers (like Android ID)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- **School Administration:** To manage student registrations, academic records, and attendance.
- **Verification:** To verify the identity of students and parents using uploaded documents.
- **Communication:** To send important announcements, academic updates, and notifications.
- **App Functionality:** To provide and maintain the Service, including troubleshooting and customer support.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information. All data, including uploaded files, is stored on secure servers. We implement commercially acceptable means of protecting your personal information. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 4. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for use by students, parents, and school staff. We strictly comply with applicable laws regarding children's data. Data regarding students under the age of 13 is collected only with the consent of a parent or guardian (via the school registration process) and is used strictly for educational and administrative purposes.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
